About Us

The story behind the most cookie-fueled team in TWBL

Who We Are

Hello, we are The Cookie Monsters. Founded in 2025 by Landon Simpson and Landon Davidson, we are a team built on competition, creativity, and a passion for the game. Our goal is to elevate wiffle ball through high-level play, engaging content, and a growing community.

We compete in the Texas Wiffle Ball League (TWBL) — Medium Pitch Division, DFW Area. Follow us on Instagram and YouTube to catch all the action.
