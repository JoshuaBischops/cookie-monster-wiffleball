About Us

The story behind the most cookie-fueled team in TWBL

Who We Are

Cookie Monster is a medium pitch wiffleball team competing in the Texas Wiffle Ball League (TWBL) out of the DFW area. Founded in 2025, we're a team of competitors who showed up to play and never left. Six players. One goal. Don't crumble.

We play out of the DFW metroplex as part of the TWBL — a league bringing competitive wiffleball to North Texas since 2012. Follow us on Instagram and YouTube to catch all the action.
